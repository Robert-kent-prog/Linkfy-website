About Linkfy

A service marketplace focused on trust, speed, and clarity.

Linkfy started to remove the friction in finding reliable service professionals. Today, we support both service seekers and providers with secure communication, verified identities, and trackable request workflows.

Our Story

Linkfy was founded after repeated challenges in finding trusted local professionals for urgent home and business jobs. The core idea was simple: make service hiring as dependable as modern banking apps.

The platform now supports end-to-end flows from discovery to negotiation, task tracking, and transparent outcomes for both sides of the marketplace.

What We Stand For

Every release is guided by reliability, user safety, and practical execution.

Trust by design

Identity verification, provider documents, and clear accountability pathways.

Faster matching

Location-aware search and category relevance reduce time to first qualified provider.

Clear communication

Real-time messaging with unread logic keeps conversations dependable and contextual.

Visible progress

Task stages and plan tracking improve clarity from request to completion.

Platform Snapshot

Capabilities already reflected in your mobile app and backend architecture:

  • OTP-based signup and recovery flows
  • Provider onboarding with ID and certification upload
  • Plan board for seeker and provider tracking
  • Inbox + chat persistence with deletion controls
  • Payments, transactions, and role-aware dashboards

Our Mission

Build the most dependable service marketplace in the region by combining secure identity, fast discovery, and transparent service execution.

Our Vision

A future where anyone can confidently find and hire verified professionals for any everyday service need.
