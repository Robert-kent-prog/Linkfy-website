About Linkfy

Connecting service seekers with trusted professionals since 2023.

Our Story

Linkfy was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to make finding reliable service professionals as easy as a few taps on your phone.

After experiencing the frustration of searching for trustworthy plumbers, electricians, and other service providers, our founder set out to create a platform that would connect people with vetted professionals in their area.

Today, Linkfy serves thousands of customers across Kenya, helping them find the right professionals for their needs while empowering service providers to grow their businesses.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves access to reliable, high-quality service professionals when they need them.

Our platform is designed to:

  • Make it easy to find trusted professionals
  • Provide transparent pricing and reviews
  • Support local service providers
  • Create a seamless booking experience
