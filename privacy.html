Legal and privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

This policy explains how Linkfy collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our app, website, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, phone number, email, role, and authentication details.
  • Service data: requests, negotiation records, chat messages, and plan statuses.
  • Provider compliance data: verification documents submitted during onboarding.
  • Technical data: device identifiers, app diagnostics, and approximate location metadata.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide account access, identity verification, and platform security.
  • To match service seekers with relevant providers by category and location.
  • To support communication, request tracking, and payment workflows.
  • To detect fraud, abuse, and policy violations.

3. Legal Basis and Sharing

We process data to deliver contracted services, comply with legal obligations, and protect legitimate business interests. Data may be shared with payment processors, infrastructure providers, and legally authorized entities when required.

4. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for service delivery, dispute handling, legal compliance, and security investigations. Retention periods vary by data category.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and review your information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion where legally permissible
  • Object to or restrict specific processing uses

Submit privacy requests via privacy@linkfy.co.ke.

6. Security Controls

Linkfy applies technical and operational safeguards, including authentication controls, role-based access, and monitored API behavior. No platform can guarantee absolute security, but we continuously improve our controls.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy, email privacy@linkfy.co.ke.
