Frequently Asked Questions

You can easily book a service provider through our mobile app or website. Simply search for the service you need, browse available providers, select your preferred one, and confirm your booking with payment.

We accept various payment methods including M-Pesa, credit/debit cards, and mobile money. All payments are processed securely through our platform.

All service providers undergo a rigorous vetting process including background checks, verification of qualifications, and customer reviews before being allowed on our platform.

You can cancel your booking through the app or website. Cancellation policies vary by service provider, but typically you can cancel up to 24 hours before the scheduled service for a full refund.

We're always looking for qualified professionals to join our platform. Visit our "Become a Provider" page to submit your application with your credentials and service details. Our team will review your application and contact you within 3-5 business days.
